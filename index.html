--- title: OntoPortal description: Ontology Repositories or Semantic Artefact Catalogues with the OntoPortal Technology ---

OntoPortal, a generic technology to build ontology repositories or semantic artefact catalogues, developped by the OntoPortal Alliance

The OntoPortal Alliance is a consortium of research and infrastructure teams dedicated to promoting the development of such repositories based on the open, collaboratively developed OntoPortal open-source software.
